How it works

Three steps to your knowledge graph

01

Feed it everything

Code repos, PDFs, Markdown docs, research papers, images, meeting transcripts. Any artifact of your working life, continuously captured.

02

Graph is built

Every entity becomes a node. Every relationship — function calls, citations, shared concepts, temporal co-occurrence — becomes an edge. Leiden clustering finds communities automatically.

03

Query anything

"What connects this meeting to that codebase?" "What did I read about pricing last month?" Answers in seconds, with sources, paths, and confidence scores.
