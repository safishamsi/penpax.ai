How it works

Three steps to your digital twin

01

Captures everything

Browser history from Chrome and Safari. Meeting transcripts. Files, emails, code, PDFs, images. Every artifact of your working day, continuously.

02

Builds the graph

Every piece of information becomes a node. Relationships -- calls, citations, concepts -- become edges. A live map of your knowledge that grows with you.

03

You query it

Ask anything. "What connects this meeting to that codebase?" "What did I read about pricing last month?" Answers in seconds, with sources.
